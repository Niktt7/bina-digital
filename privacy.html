Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PT Bina Sukses Capital ("Bina Digital", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website, applications, and services, including the "Bina Digital Bali" Instagram integration app.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Instagram username, profile information, and business account details when you connect your account through our app
  • Content Data: Posts, images, captions, and scheduling preferences that you create or manage through our platform
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services, including analytics and engagement metrics
  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number, or WhatsApp number when you reach out to us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide and improve our automation services
  • Publish and manage content on your Instagram account as authorized by you
  • Generate analytics and insights about your content performance
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Instagram & Meta Platform Data

Our app "Bina Digital Bali" accesses the Instagram API through Meta's platform. We:

  • Only access data that you explicitly authorize
  • Never sell, share, or transfer your Instagram data to third parties
  • Use Instagram data only for the purposes described in this policy
  • Store access tokens securely and delete them upon your request
  • Comply with Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements or court orders
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage, secure API connections, and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you disconnect your account or request deletion, we remove your data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Revoke app permissions at any time through Instagram Settings
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, you can:

  • Remove our app from your Instagram account settings
  • Contact us directly via WhatsApp or email (see below)

We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
